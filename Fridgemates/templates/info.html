Information about Fridgemates

About

Welcome to Fridgemates, an app to help you and your roommates to keep track of what's
in your fridge. Add items in the "INSERT" tab, and remove them on the homepage which
can be found by clicking on "FRIDGEMATES" in the top left. When editing, be sure to
select which roommate is editing, after adding the roommates of course.

Sizing System

The sizing system works on a unitary system of 1 unit being equal to a small can.

  • Extra Small = 1 Unit
  • Small = 2 Units
  • Medium = 4 Units
  • Large = 8 Units
  • Extra Large = 16 Units
